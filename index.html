Bullseye Chart

Bullseye chart illustrating WW2 battles chronologically. Greens are allied victories, reds are axis. Size of dot represents casualties.

Hover over a point to see additional data.

raphael.bullseye.js by dimarr

This is a bullseye chart made with the Raphael graphics lib. It supports mouse actions (click, hover), adding points at runtime, serializing data points, and more. Point locations can be saved and restored later.
